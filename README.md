 MyYouthspan Digital Marketing Strategy Project

A team-based digital marketing strategy project focused on **MyYouthspan**, an AI-powered personalized health and wellness product developed by MetyTechnology.

## Overview

This project examined how MyYouthspan could strengthen its market entry strategy and improve its digital presence among adults aged 40+ in the U.S. and Canada. The work focused on campaign planning, target audience analysis, channel evaluation, content strategy, and performance improvement recommendations.

## Project Goals

- Increase brand awareness in the health and wellness market
- Expand the user base through more targeted lead generation
- Improve customer engagement through educational and community-driven content

## My Contributions

- Researched the target audience, campaign geography, and outreach opportunities
- Analyzed digital channels including YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and email marketing
- Evaluated the company’s existing social media performance and identified engagement gaps
- Developed recommendations for content strategy, branding consistency, SEO, email automation, and call-to-action design
- Contributed to a full campaign report with strategic recommendations for product awareness and growth

## Key Findings

- YouTube was the strongest existing platform in terms of posting activity and visibility, but overall engagement remained low
- Existing content lacked clear product-focused messaging and strong calls to action
- The current digital presence did not fully align with the company’s goals for awareness and user growth
- A more effective channel mix should prioritize YouTube, Facebook, and TikTok for awareness, supported by SEO and email marketing for long-term growth

## Recommendations

- Improve CTA clarity across posts, videos, and emails
- Create more targeted and relatable content for the intended audience
- Use a more consistent posting strategy across platforms
- Strengthen SEO fundamentals, including metadata, alt text, and mobile optimization
- Leverage email marketing automation and A/B testing to improve lead nurturing and engagement
- Use more educational and product-specific messaging to better communicate value
